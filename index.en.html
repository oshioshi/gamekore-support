2 FAQ

Q: I cannot find a game in search.

A: We use the IGDB API, so some games may be missing. Please contact support if needed.

Q: Is my data backed up?

A: Your data is saved to Firebase Cloud. Sign in to access it on other devices.

Q: I want to delete my account.

A: Go to Settings in the app and choose Account Deletion. Related data will be removed.

Q: Can I mark a game as "Completed" without a clear ending?

A: Yes. Use your own criteria for completion (finished enough, satisfied, reached a milestone). You can change it later.
