Gamekore (the "App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use and protect it.

1 Information we collect

We collect the following information as needed to provide the service:

  • Account information (authentication details, user name)
  • Game collection information (registered games, ratings)
  • Profile information
  • Usage data (screen views, feature usage events). We do not send personally identifiable information such as email addresses to analytics.
  • Push notification tokens and device identifiers (for notification delivery and abuse prevention)

2 Purpose of use

Collected information is used only to operate the App, support users, and improve the service.

3 Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, data storage, cloud functions, analytics
  • FCM/Expo: push notifications
  • Resend: delivery of contact emails
  • IGDB: game information database

4 Sharing and disclosure

We do not provide personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  • With the user's consent
  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect life, body, or property

5 Data storage and deletion

User data is stored with appropriate security measures. After an account deletion request, related data (collections, play notes, follow relationships) and technical data such as push tokens will be deleted in sequence. Aggregated statistics that do not identify individuals may be retained for a limited period for service improvement.

6 Your rights and how to request

Users have the right to access, correct, delete, or stop the use of their personal information, including account deletion.

To exercise these rights, please contact us as follows:

  • Use the in-app Contact form and choose "Privacy"
  • Provide required details (email address, requested action, optional user name)
  • We will respond within 30 days after verifying your identity

Account deletion can also be performed from Profile settings in the App.

7 Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of personal information.

8 Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes that materially affect users (for example, the types of data collected or purposes of use), we will provide clear notice in the App or on this page. Minor adjustments such as wording or typo fixes may be announced only by updating this page.

9 Contact

For questions about personal data, please use the in-app Contact form. You may also reach us by email.

10 User choices

  • You can disable push notifications anytime in OS settings.
  • Analytics does not include personally identifiable information. If you want to opt out, please contact us.

11 International data processing

Cloud services used by the App may operate servers outside Japan. In such cases, we will apply appropriate safeguards under applicable laws.

12 Children's privacy

The App is not designed for children. Minors should use the App with a guardian's consent. We do not intentionally collect personal information from users under 13 years old (or the age defined by local law). If we learn such data was collected, we will delete it promptly. Guardians may request deletion via the Contact form.
